## What this is

A small, deliberately weird community split between a Discord server and a self-hosted website at **[degeneratefuckface.com](https://degeneratefuckface.com)**.

We exist as a counter-current to the feed-shaped internet: submissions live or die on whether they're *interesting*, not whether they're *engaging*. There is no algorithm. There is a queue, a person, and a channel that smells like menthol and ozone.

The site runs on one Dell R630 in someone's house. Zero SaaS bills. Every line of CSS was hand-written. Discord is the community spine.

## The three rooms

Pick your poison. You can wander between them.

**◈ Submissions** — drop a thing you made, in any medium: image, audio, video, text, code, link, or some unholy mixture. A human (and a bot in a trench coat) sees it before the rest of the room does. The bar is *did you make it*, not *is it good*.

**⬡ Coding feats** — long-form writeups for projects you almost can't believe you finished. Repo, demo, screenshots, post-mortem. The format that lets you brag in detail without anyone calling it cringe.

**⬢ The Discord** — where the loitering happens. The site pulses with whatever just happened in here.

## How to get in

1. Be in this server. *(You are.)*
2. Visit **[degeneratefuckface.com](https://degeneratefuckface.com)**.
3. Click **Submit a Work** — it'll send you through Discord OAuth once.
4. Your role here becomes your role there. Mods on Discord are mods on the site. Lose the role, lose the access at next login.

That's it. No credentials. No CV. Just a thing you made and the willingness to put it in a queue.

## What gets through the door

**Welcome:**
- Anything you made yourself.
- Strange hardware, ugly websites, audio you can't categorize, films of pigeons.
- Projects whose value proposition is the sentence *"why would you even build that."*
- Writeups about technically simple things that took emotionally complex weeks.

**Banned at the door:**
- Bigotry of any flavor.
- Doxxing, real or imagined.
- Illegal content.
- Generative slop you didn't edit. *(Tools are fine. Unedited copy-paste of someone else's prompt output is not.)*
- CSAM.
- Anything that wastes a moderator's time.

Past that line: **weirder is better.** Boring is a moderation reason.

## How the bot will treat you

The bot — **DegenBot#8143** — does a few things on your behalf:

- **The Doorman** — greets you in `#👋 welcome` the first time you arrive. Don't take it personally.
- **The Steno** — react to any message with **🎙** and the bot will repost it as a stamped quote in `#🎙️ quotes`. The original message gets a 📌 stamp so you know it was filed.
- **The showcase mirror** — when a submission or feat is approved, it auto-posts to `#📺 show-off` or `#⏫ code-features`, and a discussion thread opens on it.
- **Slash commands you can run** — type `/` in any channel for the menu. The headliners:
  - **`/help`** — what the bot does. ephemeral, only you see it.
  - **`/goose`** — the goose has something to say.
  - **`/wheel`** — the prize wheel airs a random approved submission.
  - **`/case-file`** — the bot DMs you a dossier of your own submissions and feats.
  - **`/receipt @someone`** — issue a cursed evidence receipt for them.
  - **`/redact <text>`** — classify a thing, publicly.
  - **`/stats`** — site and server vital signs.
